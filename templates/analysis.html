Scientific Context: Lunar XRF Analytics

X-ray Fluorescence (XRF) spectroscopy provides critical insights into planetary evolution by quantifying elemental abundances on the surface.

Geochemical Signatures

  • Lunar Maria: Characterized by basaltic flows. Look for high signatures of Fe (Iron) and Ti (Titanium).
  • Lunar Highlands: The older, heavily cratered crust composed largely of anorthosite. Marked by high Al (Aluminum) and Ca (Calcium).
Key Analytical Ratio: The Al/Si vs Mg/Si ratio is standard for classifying lunar terranes. High Al/Si typically strongly correlates with highland anorthosites.
